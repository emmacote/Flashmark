Flashmark

Let's make learning easier for everyone.

Flashmark mixes flash cards and bookmarks in a way that makes reaching your learning goals a breeze.



 
 

How It Works

Add A Flashmark

  1. Click the "Add Flashmark" button.
  2. Fill in question and answer sides within the 140 character limits.
  3. Click the "OK" button.


Your new flashmark will appear at the top of the list of your other flashmarks ready for you to review.
 

Attach Bookmarks To It

  1. Click the "resources" button next to the flashmark in question.
  2. A dialog shows up with whatever bookmarks you have for that flashmark. Push the "Add Resource" button in that box
  3. Fill in a caption and url for the learning resource in question keeping in mind the 140 character limit.
  4. Click the "OK" button.


Now, that learning resource is there for you to review any time you have the need to refresh on the ideas behind a difficult flashmark card.

Review Anywhere

Optimized for Google Chrome, Flashmark can be reviewed anytime and anywhere. Review your flashmarks at home. Review while waiting in line with your phone. Make the most of that extra convenience to give you the studying edge whatever your situation may be.
